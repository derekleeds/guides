## Why This Matters

Research shows properly structured memory improves agent retrieval by **18.5%** compared to flat vector storage. More importantly, it enables:

- **Context continuity** - Agents remember yesterday's work
- **Learning** - Knowledge accumulates over time
- **Autonomy** - Multi-day projects become possible
